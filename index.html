FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Dental Appointment Engine.

How is DAE different from a dental marketing agency?
Traditional agencies sell you leads — often unqualified names and phone numbers. DAE is a complete patient acquisition system that pre-qualifies patients via a screening quiz, nurtures them automatically, and books them directly into your calendar. You get consultations, not contact lists.
How quickly will I see results?
Most clinics see their first qualified consultation booked within the first 2 weeks. By month 2, the pipeline is typically producing consistent, predictable consultations. Results compound over time as the system optimises.
Do I need to change my existing practice management software?
No. DAE integrates with your existing calendar and practice management system. We work alongside your current tools — not replacing them.
What kind of patients does DAE attract?
DAE specifically targets patients actively searching for implant solutions. Our pre-screening quiz further filters for suitability, timeline, and budget — so you only see consultations with serious, treatment-ready patients.
Is there a lock-in contract?
No. DAE operates on a month-to-month basis. We believe in earning your business every month through results, not trapping you in a contract. You can pause or cancel at any time.
How does DAE track revenue from ads?
DAE tracks the entire patient journey — from ad click, through quiz completion, to booking, and finally to case acceptance. Our dashboard shows you exactly how much revenue each campaign and each ad dollar generates.
What kind of ROI can I expect?
Because DAE tracks the full patient journey — from ad click to case acceptance — you'll see exactly how much revenue each marketing dollar generates. ROI varies by case value, ad spend, and acceptance rate, but the revenue calculator on this page gives you a realistic projection based on your clinic's numbers.
Can I see a demo before committing?
Absolutely. We offer a free 30-minute demo where we walk you through the entire DAE system, show you the dashboard and patient flow in action, and discuss how it would work for your specific practice. No obligation, no pressure.
